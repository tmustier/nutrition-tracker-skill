# Decimo - London, UK

## Venue Overview
- **Location**: The Standard Hotel, Kings Cross, London
- **Cuisine**: Spanish-Mexican fine dining fusion
- **Website**: decimo.london
- **Style**: Premium tapas and refined desserts with emphasis on ingredient quality

## Menu Characteristics

### Philosophy & Approach
- Spanish-Mexican culinary fusion with restrained, elegant plating
- Premium/artisanal ingredient sourcing
- Traditional techniques with contemporary presentation

### Key Ingredients
- Sashimi-grade yellowfin tuna
- Cantabrian anchovies (premium European anchovies)
- Premium crab meat
- Enriched masa harina (Mexican corn flour)
- Mexican vanilla
- Guindilla peppers (Basque pickled peppers)

### Techniques
- Deep-fried Spanish pastries (empanadas)
- Raw fish preparation (tuna tostadas)
- Traditional Basque pintxo assembly (Gilda)
- Custard-based ice creams with Mexican influences
- Minimal finishing salt (especially on delicate dishes)

## Key Nutritional Learnings

### Masa Ice Cream
- Enriched masa harina contributes significant B-vitamins (B1, B2, B3) and iron
- ~200 kcal/100g (slightly lower than standard vanilla due to masa incorporation)
- Component ratio: 38% cream, 22% milk, 22% sugar, 12% masa harina, 4% egg yolk

### Gilda (Pintxo)
- High omega-3 density from Cantabrian anchovies (EPA 18mg, DHA 22mg per 22g portion)
- Traditional 3-ingredient format: 1 anchovy fillet, 1 olive, 1 guindilla pepper
- Significant vitamin A (81µg RAE) and B12 (0.9µg) from anchovy

### Tuna Tostada
- Premium sashimi-grade tuna provides high selenium (27.5µg) and B-vitamins
- Minimal sodium approach (168mg total) - no heavy salting
- High protein density (12.0g per 60g portion)

### Salt Approach
- Fine dining restraint: salt used judiciously, particularly on delicate proteins
- Tuna tostada: ~168mg sodium (minimal finishing)
- Crab empanada: ~582mg sodium (pastry dough + filling seasoning)
