## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This document provides comprehensive nutrient profiles for two ice cream desserts from Decimo London:
1. **Masa Ice Cream** (134 kcal venue-stated)
2. **Mexican Vanilla Ice Cream** (140 kcal venue-stated)

Using backsolving from venue calorie counts, USDA FoodData Central baselines, and component-based analysis, I've estimated all 52 required nutrient fields for each dessert.

---

## METHODOLOGY

### 1. Backsolving Approach
- **Starting point**: Venue-stated calorie counts (134 kcal and 140 kcal)
- **Reference baseline**: USDA vanilla ice cream (FDC #167575): 208 kcal/100g
- **Portion size estimation**: Fine dining context + calorie density analysis
- **Validation**: Atwater formula (4P + 9F + 4C_avail + 2fiber + 2.4polyols)

### 2. Data Sources
- **Primary**: USDA FoodData Central (#167575 vanilla ice cream, #20317 masa harina enriched)
- **Secondary**: Academic research on dairy omega-3/omega-6, UK dairy iodine content
- **Tertiary**: Ice cream recipe ratios (cream:milk, egg yolks, sugar)
- **Context**: Decimo London (Spanish-Mexican fine dining, Standard Hotel Kings Cross)

### 3. Key Assumptions
- **UK dairy context**: Higher iodine (30-40 µg/100g milk vs 15-20 µg/100g EU)
- **Fine dining portions**: Smaller, refined servings (65-70g typical for high-end desserts)
- **Masa ice cream**: 10-15% masa harina incorporated, reducing cream content
- **Mexican vanilla**: Premium custard base (higher egg yolk ratio)
- **Enriched masa**: Using enriched masa harina (higher B-vitamins, iron, calcium)

## PART 2: MASA ICE CREAM (134 kcal)

### Backsolving Logic

**Venue stated**: 134 kcal
**Estimated calorie density**: ~200 kcal/100g (lower than standard vanilla due to masa incorporation)
**Calculated portion**: 134 ÷ 200 = **67.0g**

#### Why Lower Calorie Density?
- Masa harina replaces some cream (lower fat content)
- Corn starch adds texture without calories
- Water content higher in masa-incorporated base
- **Rationale**: 208 kcal/100g → 200 kcal/100g represents ~4% reduction from masa dilution

#### Validation of Portion Size:
- Matches Mexican vanilla (67.3g) - restaurant likely uses consistent scoop sizes
- Fine dining portions: 65-70g is standard
- **Confidence**: HIGH (±5g)
