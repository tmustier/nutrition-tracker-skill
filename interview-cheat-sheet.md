## FIRST 90 DAYS ANSWER (30-60-90 Framework)

**Days 1-30: LEARN & DIAGNOSE**
- Shadow deals and portfolio reviews
- 1-on-1s with all partners and Operating Partners
- Visit 3-5 portfolio companies, meet management teams
- Deliverable: Present findings and 90-day operating plan

**Days 31-60: ALIGN & PLAN**
- Align on priorities with partnership
- Establish operating rhythm (monthly reviews, reporting cadence)
- Begin 2-3 deep-dive diagnostics with portfolio companies
- Deliverable: Operational systems in place, relationships established

**Days 61-90: WIN & PROVE IMPACT**
- Execute 3-5 quick wins (vendor negotiations, key hires, process fixes)
- Launch one strategic initiative (talent network, benchmarking dashboard)
- Deliverable: Measurable value delivered, credibility established

## CASE FRAMEWORKS (Draw These)

### Growth Strategy Framework
1. **Current State:** Market position, unit economics, growth rate
2. **Growth Levers:** New customers, more revenue per customer, new products/markets
3. **Constraints:** Capital, talent, technology, regulation
4. **Prioritization:** Impact vs. Effort matrix
5. **Implementation:** Roadmap, metrics, risks

### Unit Economics Framework
```
Revenue per unit
- Variable costs per unit
= Contribution margin
- Allocated fixed costs
= Unit profitability

Ask: How does this scale? Where's the leverage?
```

### Operational Efficiency Framework
1. **Map current process** (end-to-end)
2. **Measure each step** (time, cost, quality)
3. **Identify bottlenecks** (manual work, waiting time, rework)
4. **Design improved state** (automate, eliminate, simplify)
5. **Implementation plan** (quick wins first, then strategic projects)

## KEY FINTECH METRICS (Know These)

**Customer Economics:**
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC ratio (should be >3:1)
- Payback period (should be <12 months)

**Engagement:**
- DAU/MAU ratio (>20% is good for fintech)
- Retention cohorts

**Financial:**
- Gross margin (>50% SaaS, >70% platforms)
- Burn multiple (should be <1.5x)
- Take rate (revenue as % of volume)

**Operational:**
- Transaction success rate (>95%)
- Fraud rate (<0.5%)
- System uptime (>99.9%)
